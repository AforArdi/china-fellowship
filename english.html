Financial Coverage

The fellowship is comprehensive, covering both academic costs and living expenses. It combines support from the Chinese Government (Tuition, Accommodation, Stipend) and UNESCO (International Travel, Pocket Allowance).

General/Master's Stipend CNY 3,000 / mo
Senior/PhD Stipend CNY 3,500 / mo
UNESCO Pocket Allowance $80 USD / mo

Plus: Comprehensive Medical Insurance & Termination Allowance (up to $200 for excess luggage).
